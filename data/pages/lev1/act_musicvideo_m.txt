
======Activity : Music Video======
[[lev1:Act_MusicVideo_s|Ninja Instructions]] |  **Mentor Notes**

=====Purpose=====
The goals of this activity include:

  * be able to create a project the combines multiple aspects of animation and music by working on a self-directed video project
  * gain more familiarity with sprites, costumes, and sounds



=====Things to watch for=====

  * This is a mastery project so we want students to show a wide variety of learning


=====What to look for when reviewing their work=====

  * Did the projects combine both sprites and sound?
  * What parts of the project did students choose to animate?
  * Are there certain blocks or concepts introduced up until now that the student might be struggling with? How can we help them master this?
