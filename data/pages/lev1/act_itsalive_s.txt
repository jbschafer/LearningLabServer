
======Activity : It's Alive======
**Ninja Instructions** | [[lev1:Act_ItsAlive_m|Mentor Notes]]

=====Introduction=====
How can you take an image or a photo and make it come alive?\\ {{..:itsaliveSample.PNG}}
=====Duration =====
30-45 minutes

=====Instructions=====

  - Choose one of the built in sprites from scratch.\\ {{..:existingSprite.png}}
  - Click from the Scripts tab to the Costumes tab and make sure it has more than one costume.\\ {{..:costumeTab.PNG}}
  - Add blocks to make the image come alive when you run the program.\\ {{..:itsaliveStarter.PNG}}
  - Explore different values is parameters to the blocks.
  - Repeat with other sprites.
  - Try making a sprite of your own and creating multiple costumes of your own.


=====Suggestions=====

  * Explore several different sprites to see how the Scratch creators try to use costumes to enable animation. Use those ideas in your creations
  * Experiment with different blocks, block order, and costumes.


=====Things to think about when you are done=====

  * What is the difference between a sprite and a costume?
  * What is an animation?
  * List three ways you experience loops in real life.
