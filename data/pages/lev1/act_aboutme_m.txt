
======Activity : About Me======
[[lev1:Act_AboutMe_s|Ninja Instructions]] |  **Mentor Notes**

=====Purpose=====
The goals of this activity include:

  * show a familiarity with a wide range of Scratch blocks
  * be able to create an open ended Scratch project that is an interactive digital representation of the student



=====Things to watch for=====

  * This is a mastery project so we want students to show a wide variety of learning


=====What to look for when reviewing their work=====

  * Do projects make creative use of sprites, costumes, looks, backdrops, or sound?
  * Are projects interactive? Can users interact whit various elements within the project?
