
======Activity : Keeping Score ======
[[lev4:act_score_s|Ninja Instructions]] |  **Mentor Notes**

=====Purpose=====
The goals of this activity include:

  * be able to describe what a variable is and why variables are useful.
  * be introduced to the computational concept of data
  * experience remixing and reusing a project or part of a project.



=====Things to watch for=====




=====What to look for when reviewing their work=====

This game starts with a particular pattern for all games but encourages students to pursue various options.
   * Did they go beyond the base game to personalize it in some manner?
   * Can students explain what a variable is and what variables are good for?
