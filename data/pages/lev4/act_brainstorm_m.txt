
======Activity : Dream Game Brainstorm ======
[[lev4:act_brainstorm_s|Ninja Instructions]] |  **Mentor Notes**

=====Purpose=====
The goals of this activity include:

  * identifying common design elements of games



=====Things to watch for=====

  * Do the dream game lists include features of games?



=====What to look for when reviewing their work=====

  * What design elements are similar or different from each other?
  * What do the lists tell you about the kinds of games and the types of games this student will enjoy?
