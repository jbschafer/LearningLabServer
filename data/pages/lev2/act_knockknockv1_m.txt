
======Activity : Knock Knock Joke, Version 1======
[[lev2:Act_KnockKnockV1_s|Ninja Instructions]] |  **Mentor Notes**

=====Purpose=====
The goals of this activity include:

  * exploring the first of two different strategies for synchronizing interactions between sprites - timing.
  * developing a greater familiarity with the computational concept of events and parallelism
  * practice reusing and remixing



=====What to look for when reviewing their work=====

  * Did the student modify the existing code as instructed?
  * Can students explain how to adjust a program that uses timing?
