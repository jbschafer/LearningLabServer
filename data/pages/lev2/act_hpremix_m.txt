
======Activity : Harry Potter Remix======
[[lev2:Act_HPRemix_s|Ninja Instructions]] |  **Mentor Notes**

=====Purpose=====
The goals of this activity include:

  * utilize at least one of two different strategies for synchronizing interactions between sprites
  * practice the skills of reusing and remixing
  * provide an open-ended but framed opportunity to work with storytelling




=====What to look for when reviewing their work=====

  * Does the project include at least one more character?
  * Does that character have at least four lines?
  * Does this character blend into the story?
